Who should care about clean air?

Children

the developing respiratory system is particulary vulnerable to air pollution. Children growing up in polluted air are likely to have lower mental ability and more prone to depression and concentration problems. They are up to 5 times more susceptible to ADHD.

Allergy surferers

alergies are extremely onerous. They make it harder to concentrate, cause coughing, conjunctivitis, rhinits and other ailments. Do you want to get rid of allergies? Remove the cause. Clean the air from allergens.

Pregnant women

it is particularly dangerous for the health and development of a child to inhale large amounts of health hazardous PM2,5 dusts, which quickly enter the bloodstream and are distributed throughout the entire body of the mother and child.

Employees

if employees complain of problems with concentration and sleepiness, it can be a result of polluted air in the workspace.

Older people

air pollution in older people can accelerate cardiovascular and respiratory problems and even the progress of old age dementia.

You and your family

remember that you have an influence on the quality of air you breathe in your home. You and your family deserve clean air!
